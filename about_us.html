About Woof Cake Heaven

Founded in 2019 by the Ong siblings, Woofcakeheaven has been serving up woof cakes made of locally sourced, fresh, human-grade ingredients. We are fully dedicated to prepare and customise your fur kids' cake for their special occasions and celebrations!

Our special feature has got to be - DO-IT-YOURSELF ingredients menu! Choose your fur kids' favourites from our long list of food ingredients, tell us and we will create your desired cake! We guarantee that your fur kids will fall in love with our cakes the moment they see it until they finish its last bite!
