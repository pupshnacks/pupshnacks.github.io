The story of

Pup shnacks

Pupshnacks started from a simple desire to let our fur kids live their best quality of life. And it started with something simple: their food. Food without chemicals and preservatives. Food without mind-bloggling, weird-sounding ingredients.



Family

We believe that our dogs are more than just pets. They are family, a huge reason why we live a happier life. Pupshnacks is of the belief that if the food is good for human, it must be good for your fur kids.



Love & Care

Make sure your fur kids get only the best out of the best, because they truly deserve it just like our fur babies do!



Our Promise to You

We promise to provide a wide, diverse jerky menu for your fur kids to choose from and to ensure that your furkids get to obtain all the nutrients they require in a complete and balanced diet. Our shnacks can be used as a toppler for their meal or even as treats for training! Every piece of jerky goes through thorough cleaning and is made with strigent check.
