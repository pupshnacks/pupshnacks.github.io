How to store the cake?

Once received, you can keep the cake in the refrigerator until the pawty starts!

However, do note that for all ombr√© coloured frostings, the colouring might fade off and become only one shade of colour if left for hours in the fridge.

To obtain the most aesthetic image of the cake for the grams, it is best to consume the cake within 30 mins at room temperature once it is delivered to you!

All woof cakes can be served chill / at room temperature.



Overnight Storage

If you plan to keep our woof cakes overnight, they have to kept chilled or refrigerated as it is made fresh with no preservatives.

The cake may be kept for 2-3 days in the refrigerator. However, we strongly recommend to consume them on the same day it is collected / delivered.

Frosting colour may fade overnight.

You may choose to heat up the cake when serving! It brings out more aroma that way.
